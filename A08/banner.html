About Fairy Tail

Fairy Tail is one of the most powerful guilds in Fiore. Known for its strong bonds and explosive magic battles, the guild is home to unique wizards like Natsu, Lucy, Gray, and Erza.

Main Characters

Natsu Dragneel

Fire Dragon Slayer, passionate and powerful, always protecting his guild.

Erza Scarlet

The Titania of Fairy Tail, a master of Requip magic and a fierce warrior.

Gray Fullbuster

Ice-Make wizard with a cool attitude and strong loyalty to the guild.

Lucy Heartfilia

Celestial Spirit Mage who believes in friendship and teamwork.
